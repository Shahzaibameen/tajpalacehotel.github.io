About Us

Taj Palace Hotel Lahore Experience the Hospitality Taj Palace Hotel Lahore, amongst the top luxury hotels in Lahore opened its doors to visitors in the year 2000 and since then has been working tirelessly to improve services to better suit the needs and requirements of guests as they are the top priority. Taj Palace Hotel is classified as one of the top ten hotels... in Lahore by visitors due to its impeccable and well trained staff and service quality .Taj PalaceHotel consists of deluxe suites to ensure that guests are well accommodated and comfortable.

Situated at the city of heart Lahore, Taj Palace Hotel offers its valued guests top notch service, personal attention and luxurious memorable stay. It’s an oasis of calm, peace and tranquility that provides you the much needed escape from the busy, lively and bustling streets of the city. Taj Palace Hotel is a first boutique hotel that offers modern facilities seamlessly infused with epitomizing elements of elegance and royalty. Taj Palace Hotel is meditative, intimate and opulent that invites you to experience the true hospitality of Lahore. The architecture, décor & interior fuses the blueprints, colors and spirit of Lahore with contemporary design. Perfectly balanced and intriguing in every area, Taj Palace Hotel is calming to the soul and delights all those who stay here.
